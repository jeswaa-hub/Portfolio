About Me

Hello! I'm Joshua Domingo, from the little town of Villa Labrador, Rizal, Nueva Ecija, Philippines. I'm a committed 23-year-old student at PHINMA Araullo University, driven by a passion to become an exceptional front-end developer. My colorful goals and objectives, which I hold dearly, have shaped my academic journey.

I see myself as an expressive person who loves to voice ideas. I enjoy speaking honestly, whether sharing opinions on a subject or articulating concepts for a project. Following my instincts as an intuitive person has consistently helped me make judgments that serve my best interests.

Another key aspect of my personality is being engaged. I regularly participate in conversations with individuals and organizations, offering my perspectives and learning about different viewpoints. This engagement allows me to gain useful ideas from those around me, fostering continuous learning and growth.

"I am an active individual who connects the layers of life with passion, instinct, and a sincere desire to engage with and improve the world around me."
